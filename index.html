The Fundamentally
Different Approach

Traditional job sites make money when companies post jobs. We make money only when you actually get hired. This core difference changes everything about your experience.

End the Wasted Effort

  • No more black holes. Agents only get paid when you do, so they are incentivized to respond.
  • No more ghost jobs. Every role is real and actively hiring, not a resume-collection scheme from 2019.
  • Someone else sends the follow-ups. Your agent handles the "just circling back" emails so you can focus on preparing.

Gain Real Leverage

  • Create a bidding war for you. When multiple agents are competing to place you, you get more interviews and more offers.
  • The robots work for you. Instead of being filtered by bots, our agents use AI to find opportunities you didn't even know existed.
  • Negotiate from a position of power. With multiple offers on the table, you finally get to be the one to ghost the lowballers.

Keep Your Dignity

  • No more performative posting. You don't need to announce your job search journey to the world on LinkedIn.
  • Privacy is the default. Your profile is private, shared only by your agent with relevant hiring managers.
  • Focus on what matters: your skills, your interviews, and your life outside the job hunt.
